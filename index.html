Popular Bungee Places In Our Club
  • Nevis Bungy

    Go full throttle! Take on Australia's highest Bungy Jump and save heaps while doing it!

  • Nevis Swing

    Australia's World Famous Swing is on sale - and it's hardly EVER on sale! Go solo or tandem with a mate and save.

  • Nevis Catapult

    Embrace your inner superhero and take a ride on our human Catapult. It's fast, it's fun, and it's on sale!

  • Kawarau Bridge Bungy

    The OG Bungy should be ticked off every Kiwi's bucket list so we've dropped the price for a limited time, just for you!

    Parents, we got you! OUr Zipride deal is easy on the wallet and fun for the entire family.
